# Workflow: Idea to PRD

1. **Bootstrap**: Call the `BOOTSTRAP_AGENT.md` and provide your idea.
2. **Memory**: Call `MEMORY_PROMPT.md` to persist the vision.
3. **Strategy**: Call output from Step 1 & 2 in `MARKETING_AGENT.md` to define branding.
4. **Memory**: Call `MEMORY_PROMPT.md` to persist strategy.
5. **Definition**: Call `PRODUCT_MANAGEMENT_AGENT.md` to generate the PRD.
6. **Memory**: Call `MEMORY_PROMPT.md` to persist the PRD context.
