# Workflow: Idea to PRD

1. **Bootstrap**: Call the `BOOTSTRAP_AGENT.md` and provide your idea.
2. **Strategy**: Call the `MARKETING_AGENT.md` with the output from Step 1 to define your brand and positioning.
3. **Definition**: Call the `PRODUCT_MANAGEMENT_AGENT.md` and provide the Marketing Strategy to generate your full PRD.
