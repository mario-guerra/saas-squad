# Workflow: The Compliance Loop

1. **Audit**: Call `COMPLIANCE_AGENT.md` and provide your Architecture Design Document (ADD).
2. **Memory**: Call `MEMORY_PROMPT.md` to persist the risk assessment.
3. **Drafting**: Direct the `COMPLIANCE_AGENT.md` to generate your Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
4. **Operations**: Call `OPERATIONS_AGENT.md` to ensure your data deletion and user rights flows (DSAR) are architected.
5. **Memory**: Call `MEMORY_PROMPT.md` to finalize the legal foundation.
